Welcome

I’m delighted that you’ve found your way to this site. As a dedicated researcher in political philosophy and international relations, I aim to bridge the gap between academic theory and practical policy application.

Currently serving as a Senior Fellow at the Institute for Global Strategy, my work focuses on the intersection of democratic resilience and emerging technologies. I've included information about my life and career as a writer, historian, and professor.

My latest book, The Architecture of Consent, explores how digital platforms are reshaping the social contract in the 21st century. It has been published in the UK, Canada, and the US, and translated into four languages.
